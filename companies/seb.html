Ongoing project: Water to Maondo

Goal: SEK 1,000,000

Raised: SEK 721,000

Maondo is a small village north of Daadab. It has 33 families, making a total of 170 people that have to walk 9kms to the closest well.

Together with SEB you can help build a well in their village so that they can enjoy fresh water
